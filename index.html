    Experience and Research Interests
    • Nonlinear science
      • General theory of nonlinear waves and solitons.
      • Stability of nonlinear waves and solitons.
      • Self-focusing and wave collapses.
      • Theory of Hamiltonian systems.
      • Discrete solitons.
      • Dispersion-managed solitons.
      • Dissipative solitons.
      • Supercontinuum generation in optical fibres.
    • Fibre optic communications
      • High-bit-rate optical communication systems. Dispersion-management in fibre transmission lines.
      • Soliton-based technologies.
      • Design and modelling of advanced components and devices for high-bit-rate optical communication systems.
      • Statistics of nonlinear optical data transmission.
      • Information theory of nonlinear fibre channels.
      • Optical regeneration in fibre transmission systems.
    • Fibre lasers
      • Ultra-long fibre lasers.
      • Modelling and design of fibre lasers and converters.
      • Raman-based amplification in high-speed fibre links.
